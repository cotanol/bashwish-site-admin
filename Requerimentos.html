Bashwish – Full-stack Development Brief

Un resumen de los objetivos, páginas y características para la reconstrucción de la plataforma.

Goal

  • Rebuild the platform from scratch using a modern, clean codebase.
  • Deliver a mobile-first, fast-loading experience.
  • Support map search, structured filters, and vendor metadata.
  • Keep maintenance low by using serverless/simple backend tools.
  • Integrate GPT (OpenAI) for an AI assistant.
  • Set up for future monetization (e.g., per-click, featured listings, vendor dashboard).

Other Features

Party Genie (AI Assistant)

Imagine as a pop-up in the bottom-right corner for conversation.

  • Frontend form to collect party needs (age, theme, guests).
  • Integration with OpenAI (ChatGPT API) to generate a personalized party plan.
  • Suggestions include venue matches, themed add-ons, DIY ideas.
  • Display suggestions in a clean format or email to the user.
  • Optional: lead capture or feedback button.

Vendor Tools & Monetization

  • Contact form for vendors to "claim" their listing and request edits.
  • Optional vendor dashboard (for packages/images).
  • Featured listing control (admin side).
  • Basic analytics/report: views, clicks, leads sent.
  • Optional Stripe integration (for booking fee or subscription in a later phase).
  • Future flexibility for reviews, calendars, or messaging.
